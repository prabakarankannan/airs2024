AIRS 2024

06 - 12 June 2024 Karaikal, India
Join us for a high-end workshop exploring the latest in AI technologies and their applications in remote sensing.

Important Notice

All attendees are required to bring their own laptops. No registration fees are required, but an official NOC from concerned authorities is mandatory for participation.

Who Can Attend?

This workshop is designed for Ph.D., PG, and Final year UG students, and a few reserved seats are available for faculties from approved institutions and universities.


Course Outline

  • Introduction to Remote Sensing (RS)
  • Overview of Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning Models for RS
  • Deep Learning Models for RS
  • Classification and Object Detection
  • Change Detection and Monitoring
  • Multi-sensor Data Fusion
  • Land Use and Land Cover (LULC) using AI
  • Generative AI for RS, Explainable AI for RS
  • Quantum AI in RS
  • Hyperspectral and LiDAR Data Analysis
  • Operational Remote Sensing with AI

About Us



About NIT Puducherry


National Institute of Technology Puducherry (NITPY) is an autonomous public engineering institute located in Karaikal, an enclave of Puducherry. As one of India's National Institutes of Technology, NITPY holds the status of an Institute of National Importance. Established in 2010, the institute actively participates in academic and research activities, aiming to excel in science, engineering, and technology.



About the CSE Department


The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at NIT Puducherry offers robust programs at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. It boasts a curriculum that blends foundational and advanced topics, including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Sciences. With cutting-edge facilities and a dynamic academic environment, the department fosters innovation and research.



About the Workshop


This SERB-sponsored workshop, "Artificial Intelligence Models for Remote Sensing and its Applications," aims to explore and enhance the applications of AI in the field of remote sensing. The workshop includes sessions on the latest AI technologies, led by experts and scholars from prominent institutions and industries, enhancing both theoretical and practical knowledge.
